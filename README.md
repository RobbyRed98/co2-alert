# CO₂ Alert

## Material

* Arduino: ESP8266
* Multi-Color-OLED Display: SSD1331
* CO₂-Sensor: SEN-CCS811

## Wiring

| Display            |  VCC |  GND  |   SCL   |   SDA   |   RES   |   DC   |   CS   |
|--------------------|:----:|:-----:|:-------:|:-------:|:-------:|:------:|:------:|
| Arduino (Software) | 3.3V | G/GND | GPIO 14 | GPIO 13 | GPIO 15 | GPIO 5 | GPIO 4 |
| Arduino (Hardware) |      |       | D5      | D7      | D8      | D1     | D2     |
